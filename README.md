# 👗 FashionVision AI — Your Personal Outfit Classifier

> AI-powered fashion stylist that predicts your outfit category & offers custom style tips!

---

## 🔍 What It Does

FashionVision AI analyzes an outfit image and classifies it into one of five women's fashion categories:

- 👖 **Casual**
- 🧥 **Formal**
- 💃 **Party**
- 👟 **Sporty**
- 🪔 **Traditional**

You’ll also get a style confidence score and fashion tip tailored to your result.

---

## 💡 Built With
- **Python**
- **TensorFlow / MobileNetV2**
- **Streamlit** for web UI
- **PIL + NumPy** for image handling
